DIGITAL PRODUCTS

As a Tennis NSW member, you have access to a variety of digital products, including ClubSpark, Book a Court, Igloohome Locks and Barclay Lighting. These products ultimately assist with getting more people on court, whilst reducing the administration impact on you!

ClubSpark

ClubSpark is a venue management platform that tennis clubs and centres across Australia have access to utilising as part of their affiliation.

ClubSpark is an online system designed to simplify a club’s administration duties and help connect them to their participants by providing a platform that creates more opportunities to find, access and participate in tennis – more easily and more often.

Discover our online booking system – making it easier than an ever to find, book, access and pay for tennis courts and group coaching sessions.

Book a Court

Book a Court is an online court booking and payment platform that connects with pin pad technology on a club’s gate. It is easy to use for both players and club administrators, which means more time on court for everyone!

When players book a court online they are sent a PIN for direct access to their court.

Igloohome Locks

Tennis Australia has partnered with Igloohome to provide a cost-effective solution to tennis clubs for electronic gate access. The Igloohome lock is integrated with the ClubSpark software that enables players to simply book & pay online, and receive a 9-digit pin that will provide them with access to the tennis courts at the time of their booking.

Barclay Lighting

Tennis Australia has partnered with Igloohome and Barclay Consulting Services (BCS) to provide a cost-effective alternative automated booking solution to tennis clubs for gate access and lighting. The Igloohome Lock and BCS Lighting are integrated with the ClubSpark software that enables players to simply book and pay online. Users will receive a 9-digit pin for court access and an 8-digit pin for activating lights at the time of booking.
