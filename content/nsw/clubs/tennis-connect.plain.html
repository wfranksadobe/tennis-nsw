TENNIS CONNECT

What is Tennis Connect?

Tennis Connect is a series of online forums hosted by Tennis NSW. It has been designed to provide members with the latest tennis news and offer valuable support and upskilling in areas such as marketing, grant writing, venue sustainability, social media, tournament and competition hosting, plus much more!
