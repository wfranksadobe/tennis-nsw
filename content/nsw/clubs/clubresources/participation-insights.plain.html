Tennis Participation Insights

Sport Australia’s AusPlay report reveals insights into tennis participation within NSW and nationally.

AusPlay provides national, state and territory data on almost 400 different participation sport and activities in Australia, and information on who participates in them.

The infographic below summarises the key findings for tennis in NSW from July 2022 – June 2023. Click on the graphic to view and download the document in-full.
