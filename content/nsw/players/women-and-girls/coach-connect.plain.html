Coach Connect aims to engage, educate and connect women coaches across the country through mentoring and professional development opportunities.

Coach Connect Mentoring
The Coach Connect Mentoring program was launched in May 2021 on the digital platform Mentorloop for current women coach members. The objective of the program is to develop and retain current woman coaches by enabling a social environment for women coaches to come together, meet like-minded professionals and share their experiences and challenges with one another.

Coach Connect Workshops
Similar to virtual series events, workshops are in person and take a deep dive into specific areas of learning. Hands on, fun and social, our workshops are consider the hallmark events of the year with Coach Connect.

Coach Connect Networking
Coach Connect Networking events provide an opportunity for women coaches to come together to connect and network. Guest speakers or specific topics are discussed during these events.

The Coach Connect program is proudly supported by a $12 million Australian Sports Commission federal government grant.
