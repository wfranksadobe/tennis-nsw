If you’re looking for a program that’s a little more relaxed and all about having a hit, Tennis Hot Shots is the perfect option for your child.

Sessions are run by club volunteers and allow kids to have more court time with their friends. Kids will learn a heap of tips and advice and can play with their friends… or even their parents!

Tennis Hot Shots sessions are more affordable and less structured than traditional coaching sessions, and are about having fun – so don’t forget to bring your tennis shoes so that you can jump on the court too.
