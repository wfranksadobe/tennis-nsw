Tennis Australia Super 10s

The Super 10s program is a nation-wide competition for our best young tennis players aged 10 and under. In NSW, a new Super 10s competition is conducted each term. This is known as the Super 10s Finals.

Super 10s is a team competition where players play singles and doubles in a timed competition, with an on-court coach to assist players with tactical development.

Each term players can qualify for the Super 10s Finals through:

  • Maintaining a high UTR for the 2015/2016 birthyear.
  • Performance in 2024 Super 10s Competition (as a nine-year-old).
  • Playing in NSW Super 10s qualifying events.

In NSW, players who are selected to play in the Super 10s Competition, will also be invited to take part in the Super 10s Training Squad, conducted weekly at Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre, or the Metro 10s Training Squad, conducted weekly at Parramatta Tennis Centre.

There are many representative opportunities for Super 10s players. Teams are selected to play State of Origin fixtures against Queensland, ACT, Victoria, and South Australia. The best performing players will also be selected for the 2026 Australian Open Super 10s Camp, in Melbourne in January.
