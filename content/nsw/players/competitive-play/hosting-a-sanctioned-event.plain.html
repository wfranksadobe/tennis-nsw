Hosting a Sanctioned Tournament

Interested in running a sanctioned tournament?

If you or your club or association is interested in hosting a sanctioned tournament please reach out to us at nswtournaments@tennis.com.au

FAQs

How do I apply to run a sanctioned Australian Junior Tour (AJT) and Open Prizemoney (OP) tournament?
  1. You need to be a current Member of Tennis NSW
  2. Complete the Expressions of Interest form HERE
  3. Tennis NSW will review the EOI and discuss next steps with you
  4. Once approved, Tennis NSW will work with you to schedule your event on the calendar
What considerations are made when awarding tournaments?
  • Court availability and quality
  • History of hosting successful events
  • Geographic spread of events across NSW
  • Availability of qualified officials
  • Child safety measures in place
  • First aid capabilities
  • Community engagement and participation history
At what times of the year can tournaments be held?
  • School holiday periods are preferred for junior events
  • Metro and regional scheduling is coordinated to avoid clashes
What are the key timelines?
  • EOIs open mid-July each year
  • EOIs close mid-August
  • Calendar published in October for the following year
  • Tournament Directors briefing held annually
We don't have local Tournament Directors or Officials, what can we do?
  • Tennis NSW can help connect you with qualified officials
  • Visit the Officials page for more information
What other tournaments could we host?
  • Junior Development Series (JDS)
  • School Holiday tournaments
  • UTR events
  • Badge tennis competitions
  • Social leagues
Can we run a league instead?
  • Yes, leagues can be endorsed by Tennis NSW
  • Visit Tennis Australia Leagues for more information
Who can I speak to?
  • Contact your local Tennis Development Officer
  • Visit the Staff page for contact details
