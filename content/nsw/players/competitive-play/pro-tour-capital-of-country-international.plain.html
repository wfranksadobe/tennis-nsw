Pro Tour: Capital Of Country International

This September, the Capital of Country International will bring two weeks of elite Australian Pro Tour action to Tamworth. Rising stars from Australia and around the globe will compete for over AUD $60,000 in prize money and valuable world ranking points — and spectators can enjoy it all free of charge. 

EVENT DETAILS:

  • Week one: 7 – 14 September
  • Week two: 14 -21 September
  • Venue: Courts @ East
  • Cost: FREE EVENT
