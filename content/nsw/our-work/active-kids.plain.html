Important step all Active Kids providers need to take before February 2024

For your information the Active Kids program for 2024 will be changing and new applications will be required for providers (clubs, coaches and operators) to be able to continue to accept and redeem the vouchers. Applications are now open and can be completed below:

https://www.service.nsw.gov.au/transaction/apply-to-be-an-active-and-creative-kids-voucher-provider

The Active and Creative Kids voucher program will provide two $50 vouchers each calendar year to eligible parents, guardians and carers of school-aged children.

The vouchers:

  • will be available
    • twice a year, starting from February 2024
    • to eligible parents, guardians and carers receiving a Family Tax Benefit
  • can be used for eligible active or creative activities with registered providers
  • must be redeemed before the expiry date on the voucher.

Apply now to accept vouchers from February 2024

If you’re approved as an Active and Creative Kids provider, you’ll be able to accept and redeem vouchers from February 2024 through the Service NSW Business Bureau app.

If you have any further questions, please contact Service NSW on 13 77 88.

What is Active Kids?

The NSW Government is helping kids get active with the Active Kids program.

Parents, guardians and carers can apply for a voucher for each school-enrolled child in their care aged 4.5 to 18 years.

The voucher is valued at $50 and:

  • can be put towards the cost of sport and active recreation with registered Active Kids providers
  • must be redeemed before the expiry date on the voucher.

To register as an Active Kids provider and begin accepting Active Kids vouchers, first check if you’re eligible. If you are eligible, register now.
