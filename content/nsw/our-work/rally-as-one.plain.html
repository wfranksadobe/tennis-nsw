Rally as One Northern NSW Roadshow

The Australian Tennis Foundation and Tennis NSW are hitting the road across Northern NSW this December and will be hosting a number of community events. The Rally as One initiative aims to build a strong community connection in bushfire and flood affected communities, whilst reducing any associated barriers to ongoing participation. To find out more about the Rally as One program, please click here.

We’re inviting all locals to attend these free events and to enjoy a fun filled afternoon of on court and off court activities with lots of prizes to be won!

The United Cup trophy is hitting the road with us and there will be opportunities to win tickets to the event in Sydney in January with a major prize up for grabs that includes tickets, hospitality and financial travel support! For those that don’t know, the United Cup it is an ATP and WTA combined events where men and women compete for their country in mixed teams. The tournament runs from 28 December 2024 – 5 January 2025 with countries including Australia, Italy and Great Britain competing in Sydney. For more information on the United Cup, please click here.

Australian Tennis Foundation Lottery

The Australian Tennis Foundation (ATF) is excited to launch its exclusive lottery, offering tennis fans a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to experience the Australian Open in an extraordinary way.

By entering the ATF lottery, you and a friend could win tickets to your choice of men’s or women’s final, complete with flights, accommodation, on-site dining, exclusive behind-the-scenes access and special keepsakes to remember your experience.

For just $20, your lottery ticket not only gives you a chance to win this unique finals package but also helps put smiles on the faces of kids in need.

Funds raised will support the ATF deliver programs to help provide all kids with the opportunity to play and thrive through tennis, removing barriers caused by location, financial hardship or life circumstances.

Visit ausopen.com/win for full details and to purchase your tickets. Don’t miss your chance for a money can’t buy AO experience while making a positive impact in the community.

Winners will be drawn on Monday 20 January, 2025.
