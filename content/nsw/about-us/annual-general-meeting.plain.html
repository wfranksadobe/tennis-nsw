Annual General Meeting

2025 Tennis New South Wales Annual General Meeting

19 November 2025, 7:00pm AEDT

The Annual General Meeting of Tennis New South Wales Limited will be held as a hybrid meeting (that is both in person and online) on Wednesday, 19 November 2025 at 7pm (AEDT). For those wishing to physically attend in person, the AGM will be held at the Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre, 2 Rod Laver Drive, Sydney Olympic Park, NSW, 2127. Members will also have the option to view and participate in the meeting online if they would prefer.

Please see below the relevant documents relating to the AGM

  • Tennis NSW 2025 Notice of AGM Pack
  • 2025 Tennis NSW Annual Report

Members who have any queries in relation to the business of the meeting should contact the Tennis NSW Company Secretary at [email protected].
