KRA Visitor Information

Visitor information

  • Spectator entrance: The spectator entrance to KRA is located at the intersection of Olympic Boulevard and Shirley Strickland Avenue
  • Attendees are strongly encouraged to utilise public transport, it is a 10 – 15 minute walk from Olympic Park Train Station to the KRA spectator entrance. For more information regarding parking in Sydney Olympic Park please click HERE.
  • Parking: The most convenient parking station for events at KRA is P3, which is located on Sarah Durack Avenue. Parking is managed by the Sydney Olympic Park Authority and is $7 per hour or $35 flat rate all day. Please note that on busy event days, delays can be expected. Event attendees are encouraged to pre-book their parking HERE which saves time when exiting.
  • Terms & Conditions: In line with the NSW Department of Health Smoke-free Environment Act 2000 and the Smoke-free Environment Regulation 2016, smoking and the use of e-cigarettes is banned in KRA. Prams are not permitted in the precinct and there are NO facilities to store prams at KRA.
  • Upcoming Events: KRA is the official home of the United Cup, NSW Swifts and Giants Netball Teams. For more information including ticket bookings and membership, please click visit the Super Netball website
  • NSW Swifts Event Day Guide: View the event day guide here.
  • Giants Netball Event Day Guide: View the event day guide here.
