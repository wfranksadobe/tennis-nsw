The Tennis Country Management Committee is a Tennis NSW Board Appointed Sub Committee, chaired by a Director of Tennis NSW and consisting of a representative from each of the six regional committees along with at least two additional Director of Tennis NSW.

The Tennis Country Management Committee holds oversight of the six regions that make up NSW Tennis Country and the six Regional Committee that govern those regions.

The six regions that make up the Regions of NSW Tennis Country include:

North East, North West, Northumberland, Central West, South West and South East.

The Tennis Country Management Committee holds ultimate oversight into all Regional Match Play Series Events (RMS) however, RMS events are administered by each respective regional committee.

The Tennis Country Management Committee also holds oversight of the NSW Tennis Country Series events and the Closed NSW Tennis Country Championships, along with providing the final approval on the Tennis NSW Tournament and Event Calendar as it relates to events in Regional NSW.

The Tennis Country Management Committee holds final authority relating to the selection of representative players and handles any appeals that may arise from regional representative selections.
