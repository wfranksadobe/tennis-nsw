Tennis NSW Director Elections

At the 2025 Tennis NSW Annual General Meeting (AGM) to be held on Wednesday 19 November 2025, there will be two (2) Director positions up for election as a result of the expiration of the current terms of Elected Directors Andrew Mitton and Rob Nienhuis. Tennis NSW has now formally called for nominations to fill these vacancies. Nominations should be submitted by 21 September 2025.

 In accordance with the Tennis NSW Constitution, and due to the makeup of the seven (7) continuing directors, for the 2025 director elections two (2) regional classified directors must be elected. Accordingly, metropolitan classified candidates will be ineligible to be elected in 2025.

All relevant information around the director elections process, including instructions on how to nominate, is contained in a letter from Tennis NSW which was sent to each Member’s primary contact on Monday 1 September 2025. This letter, and other relevant documents, can also be accessed via the links below. It is important that Members are aware that all correspondence relating to both the director elections and the AGM will be sent to each Member’s primary contact as communicated to Tennis NSW as part of your affiliation renewal for the 2025/26 affiliation period. As a Member, it is your responsibility to ensure that you have provided the correct contact information to Tennis NSW. If you would like to update your primary contact information, or if you believe the correct primary contact has not received correspondence as expected, please contact membership@tennisnsw.com.au.

Tennis NSW recognises the importance of diversity and strives towards achieving Board representation which is reflective of the diversity of the Australian population. Accordingly, nominations from people of all backgrounds are welcomed and encouraged. In particular, due to the current composition of the Tennis NSW Board, female candidates are strongly encouraged to apply.

If you have any questions about the nominations or election processes, please contact Secretary@tennisnsw.com.au.
