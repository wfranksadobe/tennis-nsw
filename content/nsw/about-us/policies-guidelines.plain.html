TENNIS NSW MEMBER DISCIPLINE POLICY

This Policy is focused on the conduct of Members who are directly affiliated with Tennis NSW (including but not limited to affiliated Clubs, Associations and Court Operators) and sets and maintains standards of conduct and behaviour within the sport of Tennis within NSW. This Policy is not intended to cover the conduct of individual tennis participants whose conduct is properly dealt with under the Tennis Australia National Policies which can be found here. The Tennis NSW Member Discipline Policy can be accessed here.
