Hall of Fame

The Tennis NSW Hall of Fame recognises outstanding individuals (players, coaches, umpires and administrators) who have shown dedication, commitment and made a significant contribution to tennis in the state of NSW over a considerable period of time. Hall of Fame recipients have high levels of achievement in a way that contributes to the performance of athletes to the development and status of tennis as an Australian sport.
