JOIN THE ACTION – VOLUNTEER AT THE PERPETUAL NSW OPEN!

16 September 2024 | Tennis NSW

Are you interested in volunteering at this year’s NSW Open? Join our amazing team of volunteers and go behind-the-scenes to help us deliver a truly memorable event for top-tier tennis players. The 2024 NSW Open will run from Sunday 27 October to Sunday 3 November, however we are looking for enthusiastic volunteers who can start as early as Saturday 26 October.

Below are the available volunteer roles and functional areas:

  • Court Services
  • Transport
  • Ballkid Supervisor (For this position you will need to be available for training on Saturday 26 October)

Volunteers must be over the age of 15 and either have or be willing to obtain a Working With Children Check.

Applications will close Sunday 6 October at 11:59pm. Click the below link to apply!
