Join us for a day of FREE family fun at the Perpetual NSW Open this Sunday!

1 November 2024 | Tennis NSW

Join us for a day of FREE family fun at the Perpetual NSW Open Finals this Sunday, 3 November!

Kicking off at 10:00am, enjoy activities such as face painting, tennis fun, balloon twisting, and a great selection of food trucks and an outdoor bar, there’s something for everyone at this family friendly event.

In addition, Tennis World Sydney will be running a number of FREE tennis sessions for all levels and abilities. To find out more and register, please CLICK HERE.

The Men’s and Women’s Singles Finals will be played on Court 2 from 11:00am. Don’t forget to bring a seat or picnic rug to experience some thrilling tennis and an amazing atmosphere.

EVENT DETAILS:

Date: Sunday 3 November

Time: 10:00am start time

Venue: Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre

What: Face painting, balloon twisting, tennis activities, food and beverages and more!

Cost: FREE
