Tennis NSW Launches Performance Support Program

14 November 2024 | Tennis NSW

Tennis NSW is excited to launch its new Performance Support Program – an opportunity for aspiring NSW performance players of all abilities to receive financial support as they pursue higher level success.

The program is aimed at those players who have moved out of junior ranks into open level events and who are aiming to perform at a level which will lead to a higher ranking, tournament success and progressing on the world tour.

Funding support of up to $25,000 will be available to any one individual and is subject to applicants completing a written online application (stage 1) plus meeting with an assessment panel if successful in moving to stage 2.

Applications are now open and will close on Sunday 22 December at 11:59pm. For full details on the Performance Support Program, including access to the application information, click here to go to the Tennis NSW website.
