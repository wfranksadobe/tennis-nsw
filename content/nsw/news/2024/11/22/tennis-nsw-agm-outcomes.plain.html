Tennis NSW AGM outcomes

22 November 2024 | Tennis NSW

On Wednesday 20 November, Tennis NSW held its Annual General Meeting (AGM). The meeting was conducted as a hybrid meeting, with members having an option to join online or in person at Sydney Olympic Park.

This year Melissa Achten, Wayne Pascoe and Wayne Swaysland were up for re-election. Wayne Pascoe did not seek re-election. Tennis NSW would like to take this opportunity to thank Wayne for his service to Tennis NSW over the last three years, but also for his previous tenure as a Director, which included a term as President of Tennis NSW.

Wayne Swaysland, Ginette Porteous and Melissa Achten were all elected as Metro Directors, for a term of three years. Following the AGM, a Board meeting was held where Melissa was elected by the Board to continue in the President role for the next three years.

Tennis NSW would like to thank all members who voted in this year’s Director elections. The Board and Management look forward to working on the final year of the Vision 2025 Strategic Plan and delivering the next iteration of the plan in 2025.
