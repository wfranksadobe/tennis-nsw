Results Recap: Quarterfinals and Semifinals

The excitement at the 2024 Perpetual NSW Open continued to build as the men’s and women’s quarterfinals delivered thrilling matches on Friday, November 1st. Here’s a summary of the day’s results.

Men’s Singles – Quarterfinals

  • [1] Rinky Hijikata (AUS) d [LL] E. Hudd (GBR): 6-3, 6-1
    Hijikata showcased his dominance, moving swiftly into the semifinals with a convincing win.
  • [2] Thanasi Kokkinakis (AUS) d H. Shiraishi (JPN): 6-2, 6-1
  • [3] Tristan Schoolkate (AUS) d [5] O. Jasika (AUS): 6-2, 6-7(5), 7-6(7)
    In a nail-biter, Schoolkate edged Jasika in a thrilling third-set tiebreak, securing his spot in the next round.
  • [8] Yuta Shimizu (JPN) d A. Rai (NZL): 6-1, 6-4

Men’s Doubles – Semifinals

  • [2] B. Ellis (AUS)/T. Fancutt (AUS) d R. Matsuda (JPN)/A. Taylor (AUS): 6-3, 6-4
  • B. Bayldon (AUS)/M. Hermans (NED) d [4] K. Pearson (AUS)/J. Sheehy (USA): 6-3, 7-6(3)

Women’s Singles – Quarterfinals

  • [1] Talia Gibson (AUS) d [7] Petra Hule (AUS): 7-5, 4-6, 6-3
    Gibson battled through a tough match to secure her place in the semifinals.
  • [WC] Emerson Jones (AUS) d Gabriella Da Silva Fick: 6-2, 7-5
    The wildcard entrant Jones continued her impressive run with a solid win.

  • [2] Taylah Preston (AUS) d Erika Sema (JPN): 6-3, 6-4
    Preston maintained her strong form, moving confidently into the next round.
