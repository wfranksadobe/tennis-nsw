TICKETS ARE NOW ON SALE FOR THE 2024 TENNIS NSW AWARDS EVENT HOSTED BY GRAND SLAM CHAMPION CASEY DELLACQUA

6 August 2024 | Tennis NSW

Tennis NSW will welcome volunteers, administrators, players and officials from all over NSW to the 2024 Tennis NSW Awards evening, hosted by Olympian, Grand Slam champion and Broadcaster, Casey Dellacqua.

This year’s event will be held on Thursday 17 October at the Le Montage, Lilyfield. With a three-course meal, special guests, and wonderful entertainment, we welcome all members of the tennis community to join us in celebration from 6:00pm. There are a limited number of tickets available so to ensure your spot, get in quick!

EVENT DETAILS
Date: Thursday 17 October
Time: 6:00pm – 10:30pm
Venue: Le Montage, Lilyfield
