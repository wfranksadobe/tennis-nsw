Congratulations to our 2024 Tennis NSW Awards winners

18 October 2024 | Tennis NSW

Last night, Tennis NSW hosted the 2024 Tennis NSW Awards ceremony, where many amazing members and stakeholders of the NSW tennis community were recognised and celebrated.

The Awards were hosted by Grand Slam champion, Olympian and Broadcaster, Casey Dellacqua at Le Montage in Lilyfield. The memorable evening honoured the 90th birthday of eight-time Grand Slam champion, Ken Rosewall, and celebrated the induction of Todd Woodbridge into the Tennis NSW Hall of Fame.

Tennis NSW would like to congratulate all 2024 Awards winners!

2024 AWARD WINNERS

  • NSW Player of the Year | Jordan Thompson
  • Junior Athlete of the Year – Female | Renee Alame
  • Junior Athlete of the Year – Male | Cruz Hewitt
  • Most Outstanding 30+ Tennis Master | Igor Jovanovic 
  • Young Athlete with a Disability | Arato Katsuda-Green
  • Most Outstanding Athlete with a Disability | Grace Hobbs & Michael Leigh 
  • Young Volunteer Achievement | Asha Munodawafa
  • Volunteer Achievement | Pauline McIlveen
  • Most Outstanding School | Meriden School
  • Most Outstanding Tournament | Albury Tennis Association Margaret Court Cup
  • Coaching Excellence – Club | Benjamin Jones
  • Coaching Excellence – Development | Kelsey Lewis
  • Excellence in Venue Operations | City Community Tennis 
  • George Fletcher Award – Excellence in Officiating | Matthew Kellert
  • Senior Officiating Award | Matthew McAleer
  • Most Outstanding Tennis Club or Venue – Country | Total Tennis Lake Macquarie
  • Most Outstanding Tennis Club or Venue – Metro | Tennis Macarthur – Wests Tennis Centre
  • Tony Podesta Most Outstanding Inclusion Initiative | City Community Tennis 
  • Jim O’Neill Service to Tennis | Elva Osborne

Congratulations once again to all of our incredible Award recipients, the tennis community thanks you for your efforts and continued dedication to tennis over the past 12 months.
