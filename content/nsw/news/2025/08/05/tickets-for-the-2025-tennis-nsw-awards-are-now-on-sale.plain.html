Tickets for the 2025 Tennis NSW Awards are now on sale!

5 August 2025 | Tennis NSW

Join us for a special evening on Thursday 16 October 2025 at Le Montage, where we will celebrate the incredible achievements of our players, coaches, officials, clubs, schools, and volunteers who have gone above and beyond to ensure tennis continues to thrive in NSW.

With a three-course meal, special guests, and wonderful entertainment, we welcome all members of the tennis community to join us in celebration from 6:00pm. There are a limited number of tickets available, so to ensure you secure your spot, get in quick!
