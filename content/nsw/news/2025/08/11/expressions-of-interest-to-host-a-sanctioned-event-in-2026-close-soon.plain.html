Expressions of interest to host a sanctioned event in 2026 close soon!

11 August 2025 | Tennis NSW

We are excited to announce that eligible venues affiliated with Tennis NSW are invited to express their interest in hosting a sanctioned tournament in 2026.

Key requirements:
Venues must ensure they have the capacity to effectively deliver Australian Junior Tour and Open Prizemoney tournaments. This includes having an adequate number of courts and available tournament personnel.

How to apply:
Please complete the EOI form by clicking the button below. Once the EOI has been submitted, Tennis NSW will use the information provided to commence building the 2026 tournament calendar. During this process, successful tournament hosts will be contacted to formally apply for their tournament in 2026.

Important notes:

  • Submitting an EOI does not guarantee your venue a tournament.
  • For more information on the sanctioning process and FAQs, please visit our website here.

If you have any questions, or require assistance, please contact your local Tennis Development Officer.
