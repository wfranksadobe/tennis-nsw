Easter 2025 was a smashing success for tennis in NSW!

6 May 2025 | Tennis NSW

Over the Easter weekend, thousands of players across the state jumped on court to compete at annual Easter tournaments in Albury, Nepean and Coffs Harbour.

With over 100 events played across three venues, the long weekend provided competitive playing opportunities to over 1000 players.

Tennis NSW Head of Tennis Operations & Pathways, Shaun Mulraney, stated, “Participation in our Easter tournaments was outstanding. Despite the cost-of-living challenges in 2025, our numbers continue to increase. Our tournament deliverers, officials, and volunteers did a fantastic job delivering numerous events across the state. Now, we are excited and gearing up for the action-packed July Swing!”

A massive congratulations to all the players who competed over the Easter weekend and to all the officials, volunteers, parents, coaches and tournament staff.
