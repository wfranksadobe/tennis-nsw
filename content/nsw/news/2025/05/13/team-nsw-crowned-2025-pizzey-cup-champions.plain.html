Team NSW crowned 2025 Pizzey Cup champions

13 May 2025 | Tennis NSW

Congratulations to Team NSW who have secured the 2025 Pizzey Cup title! The prestigious national secondary schools event took place from Saturday 3 May – Saturday 10 May in Bendigo, Victoria.

The Pizzey Cup is a distinguished national tennis championship for secondary school students, allowing boys and girls aged 18 and under to represent their state in teams of eight players. Competing against the finest talents from across Australia, this event showcases exceptional young players.

In an exciting final, Team NSW clinched the title with a one set victory over Team VIC after both teams were tied at nine rubbers.

Congratulations to our incredible NSW players, along with the supportive parents, coaches, and schools.
