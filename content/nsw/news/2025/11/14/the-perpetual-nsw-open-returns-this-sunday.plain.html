The Perpetual NSW Open returns this Sunday

14 November 2025 | Tennis NSW

The Perpetual NSW Open is back with a star studded Aussie line-up of men and women. The Australian Pro Tour event is a combined Men’s ATP75 Challenger Tour and Women’s ITF World Tennis Tour W75 and will take place from the Sunday 16 November – Sunday 23 November at Sydney Olympic Park. Entry is FREE all week, so come down and enjoy the action courtside!

With valuable world ranking points on offer and a total prize money pool of $100,000 USD for men and $60,000 USD for women, the event attracts a strong local and international field.

This year’s men’s field includes NSW’s own Christopher O’Connell, James Duckworth and 2024 runner-up Rinky Hijikata, while on the women’s side defending champion Emerson Jones will be joined by Great Britain’s former United Cup representative Katie Swan and NSW’s Gabriella Da Silva Fick.

Don’t miss a moment of the action! Head to Sydney Olympic Park and watch our top players battle it out for the 2025 title – all for FREE!
