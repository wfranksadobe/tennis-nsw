Expressions of Interest to fill vacant Metro Sydney and Regional Committee positions now open

4 November 2025 | Tennis NSW

The Tennis NSW Board is calling for expressions of interest to fill vacant positions on either the Metro Sydney Committee or Regional Tennis Committees.

The currently vacant positions are as follows:

  • General Committee Member – Metro Sydney Committee (multiple positions)
  • Chair – Northumberland regional committee
  • Treasurer – South East regional committee
  • Treasurer – Central West regional committee
  • Treasurer – Northumberland regional committee
  • Treasurer – North West regional committee
  • Treasurer – North East regional committee

It is expected that persons expressing interest in filling the above positions will be active participants in the region relevant to the role applied for.

The Metro Sydney Committee is a sub-committee of the Tennis NSW Board, whilst the Regional Committees are sub-committees of the Tennis Country Management Committee (Tennis Country) and are established to work alongside Tennis NSW management to consider and deliver competitive playing opportunities for the entire tennis community, but with a focus on their specific region. These committees act as advisory bodies to the Tennis NSW Board and work closely with Tennis NSW management to provide strategic advice on, and deliver, a range of objectives including but not limited to a strong competition framework and culture across metropolitan and regional NSW. Participation on these committees is voluntary and will not be remunerated.

The Tennis NSW Board is looking for individuals with demonstratable skills relevant to the position i.e. previous experience chairing a committee or similar and/or relevant financial qualifications or experience. The Board also considers it desirable that persons on the Tennis NSW committees will have varied skills and/or experiences in tennis in the relevant regional context e.g. playing experience, club involvement, experience delivering tournaments/events, or experience running or managing a tennis venue. Individuals with diverse backgrounds of all kinds are welcome and encouraged to apply.

If you would like to contribute to the direction of tennis in your region, please complete an expression of interest by clicking the link below to access the EOI form.

Expressions of interest will close 16 November 2025.
