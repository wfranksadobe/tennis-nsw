Kangaroo Valley Public and The Illawarra Grammar School take home the 2025 Todd Woodbridge Cup

12 December 2025 | Tennis NSW

Over 300 students gathered at the Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre and Parramatta City Tennis Centre for the Stage 2 and Stage 3 Todd Woodbridge Cup State Finals. The event was the culmination of months of zone and regional events designed to grow the sport at the grassroots level.
 
This primary school tennis competition encourages participation from all skill levels, providing a positive and inclusive experience for those in years 3 and 4 (Stage 2) and years 5 and 6 (Stage 3).

The Todd Woodbridge Cup has grown remarkably, from a competition of just over 1000 players to an event that now provides more than 9000 students across NSW with the opportunity to experience competitive tennis.

A big congratulations to Kangaroo Valley Public School, who defended its Stage 2 title, and to The Illawarra Grammar School, our Stage 3 champions. Both State Final events were a true celebration of tennis, creating unforgettable moments and giving young players the chance to shine in high-level competition.
