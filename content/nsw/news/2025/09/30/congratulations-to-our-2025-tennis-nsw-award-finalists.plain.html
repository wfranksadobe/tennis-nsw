Congratulations to our 2025 Tennis NSW Award Finalists

30 September 2025 | Tennis NSW

Tennis NSW are delighted to announce the finalists for the upcoming annual Tennis NSW Awards in 2025. The Awards recognise clubs, organisations and individuals in the NSW tennis community. These awards align with the national Newcombe Medal, Australian Tennis Awards. Winners of a Tennis NSW Award, for which there is an equivalent Newcombe Medal, Australian Tennis Awards category, will be the Tennis NSW nominee for that category.


We are thrilled to invite the entire tennis community – volunteers, administrators, players, coaches and officials – from all across the state to join us at the 2025 Tennis NSW Awards. The event will be co-hosted by Olympians and Grand Slam champions Casey Dellacqua and John Fitzgerald, bringing together the tennis community to acknowledge and reward the outstanding performances and positive contributions within the NSW tennis community.

AWARD CEREMONY DETAILS:
When: Thursday 16 October
Time: 6:00pm – 10:30pm
Where: Le Montage, Lilyfield
Dress code: Cocktail
Event type: 3-course gala dinner including beer, wine & soft drink
Tickets: $175pp or $1,650 for a table of 10. Tickets can be purchased here
