BADGE 2025 RECAP

9 September 2025 | Tennis NSW

After a fantastic season (and unseasonably wet August) our 2025 Sydney Badge Champions have been crowned. The 14-week competition culminated in a fantastic finals day which saw Roseville and Tennis World Ladies compete for the Saturday Women’s Div 1 title while Manly Men 1 and Hills District 1 competed for the Men’s Div 1 title.  Teams fought hard with several matches going down to the wire. A single game saw Hills District 1 victorious over Manly Men 1 on their home courts winning 2 rubbers, 4 sets and 38 games to Manly’s 2 rubbers, 4 sets and 37 games.  Unfortunately wet weather meant the Thursday Ladies competition was won on ladder placement with both semis and finals washed out. 

Congratulations to all our teams and we look forward to seeing you on court again for Badge in 2026!
