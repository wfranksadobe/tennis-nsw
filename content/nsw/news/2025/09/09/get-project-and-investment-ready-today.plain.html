Get Project and Investment Ready Today!

9 September 2025 | Tennis NSW

If you’re planning court resurfacing, lighting upgrades, accessibility improvements, or any other infrastructure project , now is the perfect time to get Project and Investment Ready. 

Two upcoming grant opportunities could provide valuable funding support for these infrastructure projects. 

Get started today by contacting your local Tennis Development Officer – They’re here to help!

Don’t forget to sign up for the Project and Grant Ready Toolkit, packed with valuable resources to support your project planning.

Project and Grant Ready Toolkit Sign Up Form



Clubgrants Category 3 – Infrastructure Grants (sport and recreation)

The Clubgrants Category 3 program provides grants between $50,000 and $300,000 to support construction, alteration, renovation, completion and fit-out of buildings that deliver benefits for disadvantaged NSW communities.

Application opens: 24 November 2025

Application closes: 15 December 2025

For more information please see Grant website – Infrastructure Grants: sport and recreation | NSW Government


Community Building Partnership (CBP) – 2026 Round

The CBP 2026 round dates will be announced soon, Sign up to the Project and Grant Toolkit to receive updates as soon as the dates are released!

This NSW Government program awards grants for community infrastructure projects that deliver positive social, environmental and recreational benefits while fostering inclusion and cohesion.

CBP grants are typically between $10,000 and $100,000 and supports projects including capital works, equipment purchases, and vehicle acquisitions in NSW.

To find out more about the grant, visit their website –  Community Building Partnership | NSW Government
