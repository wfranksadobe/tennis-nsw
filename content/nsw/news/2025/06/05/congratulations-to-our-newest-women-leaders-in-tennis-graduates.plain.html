Congratulations to our newest Women Leaders in Tennis graduates

5 June 2025 | Tennis NSW

Tennis NSW proudly welcomed 19 inspiring women to Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre on Sunday 25 May for an afternoon of learning, collaboration, and connection.

The Women Leaders in Tennis program empowers women to step into leadership roles within their communities through mentorship, professional development, and networking. Strong female voices are vital—they help shape inclusive environments, influence local decision-making, and enhance the playing experience for women and girls.

Since launching in 2022, over 540 women across Australia have taken part in this program, helping to build a strong pipeline of future leaders.
