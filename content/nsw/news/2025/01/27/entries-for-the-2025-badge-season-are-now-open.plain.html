Entries for the 2025 Badge season are now open!

27 January 2025 | Tennis NSW

The Badge league is back and entries for the 2025 season have officially opened!

As we gear up for another exciting Badge season, please take note of the important information and key dates outlined below.

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

  1. Entry process: Team entries should be submitted through Match Centre HERE. Each team must enter a minimum of four base players, with a maximum of 12 base players. More information on how to enter your team can be found HERE.
  2. Entry deadline: Entries will close on Sunday 23 February at 11:59pm.
  3. Competitive Player Profile (CPP): This season, all players will require an updated CPP. This was previously referred to as Match Centre. If you do not have a CPP, or are uncertain about your status, please CLICK HERE. Please also note that it is important to check your CPP is linked to your UTR and ensure you have merged any duplicate profiles you may have.
  4. Reserve nominations: Open and closed reserve nominations will open in mid-February. Visit the Badge website HERE for more details.

KEY DATES

Head to our Badge website HERE to find out more, including entry links, factsheets, reserve player information and more.
