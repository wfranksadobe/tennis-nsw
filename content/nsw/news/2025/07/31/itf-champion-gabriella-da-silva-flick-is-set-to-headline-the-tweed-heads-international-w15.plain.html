ITF champion, Gabriella Da Silva Flick is set to headline the Tweed Heads International W15

31 July 2025 | Tennis NSW

ITF Singles champion and hometown hero Gabriella Da Silva Flick (NSW) will lead the women’s field at the back-to-back Tweed Heads International W15 events held at the Tweed Heads Tennis Club.

Event dates:

  • Week One: Sunday 3 August to Sunday 10 August
  • Week Two: Sunday 10 August to Sunday 17 August

The Australian Pro Tour event, is a women’s ITF World Tennis Tour W15 tournament, offering a total prize pool of USD $30,000, along with valuable world ranking points.

Da Silva Flick aims to secure her second ITF title, having previously won her first title at the Swan Hill International W35 event in 2024.

The 2025 Tweed Heads International W15 is set to showcase an impressive lineup of Aussie talent, featuring notable Aussies, Alana Subasic, Belle Thompson and Lily Taylor, as well as Alicia Smith, and Laquisa Khan.
