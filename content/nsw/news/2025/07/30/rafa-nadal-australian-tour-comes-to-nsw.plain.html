Rafa Nadal Tour hits NSW

30 July 2025 | Tennis NSW

The 2025 Rafa Nadal Tour arrived at Sydney Olympic Park Tennis Centre, from 13–15 July, bringing together the top junior tennis players from across New South Wales.

More than just a tournament, the Rafa Nadal Tour encompasses high-level competition and personal development, guided by four core values: humility, resilience, commitment, and sportsmanship.

Across three action-packed days, players competed on court while also taking part in off-court values sessions designed to foster personal growth. These engaging activities are a key feature of the tour, reinforcing the belief that success in sport is just as much about character as it is about performance.

Those who consistently demonstrated these values were recognised with the prestigious Trophy of Values—an award that highlights attitude, behavior, and leadership both on and off the court.

The event crowned winners across four singles categories, with each champion, along with the Trophy of Values recipients, earning a coveted spot at the Rafa Nadal Tour Australian Masters, set to take place in Melbourne in January 2026. There, they’ll compete for the ultimate prize: an all-expenses-paid, two-week training experience at the world-renowned Rafa Nadal Academy in Mallorca, Spain.
