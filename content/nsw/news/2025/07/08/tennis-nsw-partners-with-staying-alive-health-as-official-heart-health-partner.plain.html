Tennis NSW partners with Staying Alive Health as official Heart Health partner

8 July 2025 | Tennis NSW

Tennis NSW is proud to announce Staying Alive Health as its official Heart Health partner. This collaboration marks a significant step forward in ensuring that all tennis venues across NSW can provide safe environments for all individuals engaged in the sport.

Staying Alive Health is the exclusive distributor of the Rotaid 24/7 defibrillator cabinet in Australia and New Zealand, which is Europe’s top-selling fully monitored option. Additionally, SAH distributes leading defibrillator brands such as ZOLL and Heartsine.

Through this partnership, affiliated tennis venues in NSW will gain access to defibrillators, training, and ongoing support at a discounted rate, along with comprehensive grant application support.

Tennis NSW CEO Darren Simpson expressed, “We are thrilled to be partnering with Staying Alive Health as our official Heart Health partner. Tennis NSW is dedicated to ensuring the safety of every individual in our tennis community and recognises that this partnership will equip our affiliated venues with essential education and access to state of the art first-aid equipment to achieve this.”

“We’re incredibly proud to partner with Tennis NSW to raise awareness of heart health and ensure clubs across the state are ready to save a life if called on. Tennis is a sport for all ages, and with so many clubs and players across NSW, it’s vital that every venue is equipped and prepared. This partnership is about more than just equipment, it’s about empowering communities with the tools, knowledge, and confidence to act when every second counts,” stated Ben Collins, Managing Director of Staying Alive Health.

By collaborating, Staying Alive Health and Tennis NSW are committed to fostering safer communities and environments for all players, volunteers, spectators, and community members, ensuring that tennis remains a sport for life.
